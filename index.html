Meet BillKadai

BillKadai is Nowhere Origin's first product — a billing and POS app built specifically for small businesses across India.

We built it because shop owners shouldn't need expensive software, stable internet, or technical knowledge to run their business. BillKadai works offline, generates UPI QR codes, supports GST, and runs in 5 languages.

It is completely free to download and use. No subscription. No hidden charges. Ever.

Features

Everything your shop needs.

From billing to sales reports — BillKadai handles the complete business flow, even without internet.

⚡
Bill in 5 seconds
Tap items, set quantity, generate bill. No typing, no delays. Designed for busy shops with high customer volume.
📶
100% Offline
No Wi-Fi or mobile data needed. Works during power cuts, in basements, in rural areas — wherever your shop is.
💰
UPI QR Payments
A UPI QR code is auto-generated on every bill. Customers scan and pay instantly with any UPI app — PhonePe, GPay, Paytm.
🧾
GST Support
Inclusive and exclusive GST calculation built in. Professional invoices with tax breakdown on every receipt.
📊
Sales Reports
Daily, weekly and monthly reports. Cash vs UPI breakdown, top items, and revenue summaries at a glance.
☁️
Cloud Backup
All your data is automatically backed up. Restore everything on a new phone in seconds — nothing is ever lost.
👥
Staff Management
Add multiple staff members with individual PIN access. Control what each person can see or do in the app.
📤
Share Bills Instantly
Send bills via WhatsApp, SMS, or any app with a single tap. Customers receive a clean digital receipt.
🔒
Secure & Private
Your business data stays on your device. No third-party access. PIN-protected with optional cloud backup.
How It Works

Up and running
in 3 steps.

No training required. No technical knowledge needed. If you can use a phone, you can use BillKadai.

1
Download BillKadai
Install for free from Google Play Store. No account required. Open the app and you're ready.
2
Set up your shop & menu
Add your shop name, items, prices, and UPI ID. Takes about 5 minutes. Change anything anytime.
3
Start billing customers
Tap items → bill generated → share via WhatsApp. All in under 5 seconds. Works entirely offline.
